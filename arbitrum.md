# Why Ondo USDY is the Superior Collateral for the Arbitrum Agentic Economy

## 1. Liquidity & Ecosystem Integration
Ondo Finance natively issues USDY onto Arbitrum via LayerZero OFT and native smart contracts. This means immediate, deep liquidity is available without relying on cumbersome, low-liquidity bridges for geographically limited instruments like CETES.

## 2. Global Accessibility
A software-only proxy like Stylo Protocol must remain globally accessible and trustless. Using US Treasuries as the baseline yield provides a universal standard of value that agents can reliably calculate Delta-Neutral positions against, unlike the volatile MXN/USD exchange rate dependency intrinsic to CETES.

## 3. DeFi Composability
GMX v2, Pendle, and Pyth Oracles natively support USD-denominated stable yielding assets. By anchoring Stylo’s AI swarms in USDY, they can execute atomic flash-loans, multi-leg arbitrage, and complex structured products natively on Arbitrum without latency-heavy cross-chain checks (which CETES would require to verify balance across the OFT bridge).
